TIME WARNER CABLE AD

Write Up:

The most important part of this project for me was the font choice. I chose the font because I wanted something clean but with a modern flare to give the company a newly refreshed look for the customers they are trying to get to rejoin their services.

The heading and subheading are meant to peak interest and convince the user to want to learn more. I kept the iconography aligned with that on their website to give the ad and brand in an overall cohesive feel. The potential client can then click in any of the icons to go to a page on their site that describes each service in depth.

The “find a package” form was purposely given a very clean look in order to provide the potential client with comfort while providing their personal information.

The overall look is clean to give the customer a sense that the company has been refreshed since they last left their account. It is also friendly enough to entice new clients to inquire about the services the company provides.
